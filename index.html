Trade Show Coming Up?

Trade Show Coming Up? A successful booth is one that has traffic. Magic is a great way to get people's attention, and keep them at your booth for sales presentations. In addition, customized presentations can be created using your company logo, product, or product message.

Corporate Banquet This Evening?

Most employees are great at communicating one on one, or in a small group. But how about in front of a large audience? A Magician can entertain and emcee; and by using selected employees, turn simple tricks into hilarious audience effects.

Hosting a Party?

Has your standard entertainment (pin the tale on the donkey, home videos, etc.) become "old"? Close-up Magic provides a terrific alternative. Using cards, coins, rings (and other small items), the magic happens in your guests' hands - creating something truly amazing.

A Two Hour Wait for Dinner?

"The food may be worth the wait, but do I have to stare at this red velvet wallpaper until dinner" Sound familiar? A strolling (or table-hopping) Magician can provide personal entertainment, making the time go by faster. And, if a large group is seated, the Magician can stroll by at pre-set times, helping the servers with the extra time required.

Team Building

Looking for a new way to introduce Products or Services? To enliven your normal "Monthly Meetings"? To energize your Staff? By interweaving your Company "Buzzwords" with the art of Prestidigitation, mundane Presentations prove truly entertaining (and maintain attention span).
